Currently setting out on a new adventure as a Digital Services Developer for the city government of Asheville, North Carolina. Always following my curiosity.

My primary interest lies in ethically, transparently, and compassionately easing state and local governments into the twenty-first century. Along the way, I hope to study collaborative governance in technical projects, security risks of using open source and free software in government, whether procurement processes can be fixed with lessons from human-centered design, what data science can teach us about economics, and other topics at the intersection of technology and public service.

Previously, I visualized data for a cybersecurity startup, earned a degree in trumpet performance, traveled over seven thousand miles by bicycle, and worked in several educational roles.

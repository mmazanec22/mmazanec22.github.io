Ostrich Farms

Ostrich Farm Locations

There are approximately 345 ostrich farms in the United States. The majority of them are in Arizona. Ostriches hate the desert and prefer areas with more sustainable water supplies, but they do enjoy a nice vacation to the Grand Canyon every now and then. The best way to find an ostrich farm is by hitchhiking.

Why Ostriches

Ostriches are raised primarily for their eggs. Studies have shown that eating a humongous sunny-side up ostrich egg is even better for you than slathering yourself in coconut oil and doing yoga.
